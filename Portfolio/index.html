Welcome

Ghana is a peaceful country in West Africa known for its rich culture, history, hospitality, and gold.

Ghana gained independence in 1957 — the first African country to gain independence.
Home to top universities like University of Ghana and KNUST.
Visit Cape Coast Castle, Kakum National Park, Osu Castle, Elmina Castle, and Lake Volta.
Enjoy dishes like jollof rice, banku, waakye, fufu, dzemple, yakeyake, amotu, and fufu.
